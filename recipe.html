Recipe Details

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients: Pasta, eggs, cheese, bacon.

Instructions: Boil pasta, mix with scrambled eggs and cheese, add cooked bacon.

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients: Paneer, butter, cream, tomato gravy.

Instructions: Cook tomato gravy, add paneer and cream, simmer in butter.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients: Bread, cheese, butter.

Instructions: Butter bread, place cheese between slices, grill till golden brown.

Veg Biryani

Ingredients: Basmati rice, vegetables, spices.

Instructions: Cook rice and vegetables separately, layer with spices, steam together.

Mango Smoothie

Ingredients: Mango, yogurt, honey.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients till smooth, serve chilled.

Omelette

Ingredients: Eggs, onion, green chili, salt.

Instructions: Beat eggs with chopped veggies, cook on pan till fluffy.
